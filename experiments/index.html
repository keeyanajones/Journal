These experiments are carried out to support, refute, or validate a hypothesis. They provide insight into cause-and-effect by demonstrating what outcome occurs when a particular factor is manipulated. These vary greatly in goal and scale, but always rely on repeatable procedure and logical analysis of the results. The success logs, things to revisit, goals, and lessons learned provided.
