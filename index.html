Overview

My journal addresses issues of computer applications, information and communication systems, software engineering and management, CAD/CAM/CAE, numerical analysis and simulations, finite element methods and analyses, robotics, computer applications in multimedia and new technologies, computer aided learning and training. Objectives

The objective of the journal is to help professionals working in the field, educators and policy makers to contribute, to disseminate information and to learn from each other|s work. This Journal also aims at establishing channels of communication between consultants, myself, academics, computer manufacturers, software developers and users worldwide.

Readership

Professionals, academics, researchers, and managers.
