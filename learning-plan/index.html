Learning Plan

Last updated on October 2nd, 2019

  1. Home
  2. Learning Plan

School after a decade of practical experience forced me to learn how to learn, stay relevant, and become effective as a lifelong learner. My learning plan also taught me to :

  • Value failure
  • Focus on process, not outcome, and on questions, not answers
  • Make time for reflection
  • Learn to be true to myself by playing to my strengths
  • Pair specialization with variety
  • Treat others as learning partners
  • Enjoy the process

Planning always helps in trying to reach goals. I have goals I am working to achieve within 2020. These have been divided into more manageable sub-goals that can be realized within weeks and/or months. The planned series of concrete steps and/or actions I am taking:

  • Establish the purpose/direction
  • Identify development needs
  • Look at development opportunities
  • Formulate action plan
  • Undertake development
  • Record outcomes
  • Review and evaluate

To record outcomes and evaluate resources with evidence:

  • Live Streams
  • Labs
  • Field Reports
  • Experiments

Resources

The plan is to post anything that was used to realize the actions to be taken towards the goal. This includes mentors or coaches comments, books, courses, and the Internet especially sources and Youtube. Once a resource has been applied to a goal rate the effectiveness of the resource and write an article.
